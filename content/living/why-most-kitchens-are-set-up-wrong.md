Most kitchens aren't lacking tools.

They're lacking flow.

---

Too many single-use items.

Too much clutter.

Cooking becomes friction.

---

A good kitchen removes thinking.

You reach. You cook. You move.

---

A good kitchen doesn't look full.

It feels usable.
