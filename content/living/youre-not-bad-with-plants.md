There's a common belief that some people are "plant people" and others just aren't.

It's not true.

Most people don't fail at plants. They just skip understanding them.

---

## What plants actually do

They change a space.

The room softens.

The air feels lighter.

The space feels alive.

---

## The real problem

People ignore:

- light
- environment
- habits

Then blame themselves.

---

## The better approach

Match the plant to your environment.

---

## Low light

Snake Plant  
ZZ Plant  
Pothos

---

## Bright indirect

Monstera  
Rubber Plant  
Fiddle Leaf Fig

---

## Direct light

Olive Tree  
Succulents  
Herbs

---

## Orchids

Not disposable.

Repot with orchid bark.

Give indirect light.

They will bloom again.

---

## The takeaway

You don't need to be good with plants.

You need the right setup.
