There's a difference between something that feels good for five minutes  
and something that actually works.

Most people buy blankets for the first one.

That soft, synthetic, plush throw feels great in the store.

It feels great when you first put it on.

And then, almost instantly, it doesn't.

---

## The problem

Synthetic blankets trap heat.

They don't breathe.

They don't regulate.

One minute you're comfortable.

The next, you're overheating and trying to kick it off.

---

## The alternative

100% cotton.

Not a blend. Not "cotton feel."

Actual cotton.

---

## Why it works

Cotton breathes.

It lets air move.

It adjusts with your body.

You stay comfortable.

No spikes. No sudden overheating.

---

## It gets better over time

Good cotton blankets improve.

After a few washes:

- softer
- more broken-in
- more comfortable

---

## About the lint

The first few washes will look aggressive.

Lint everywhere.

That's not a flaw.

That's cotton settling into itself.
