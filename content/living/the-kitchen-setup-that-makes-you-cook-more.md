Cooking more isn't discipline.

It's environment.

---

If your kitchen is easy, you cook.

If it creates friction, you don't.

---

Keep:

- knife
- board
- one good pan

Everything else stays out of the way.

---

You're not optimizing for aesthetics.

You're optimizing for repetition.
