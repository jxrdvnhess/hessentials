Most people are washing their clothes correctly.

They're just finishing them wrong.

---

Fabric softener feels like part of the process.

It smells clean.

It sounds like it's helping.

It isn't.

---

## What it actually does

Fabric softener coats your clothes.

That coating:

- reduces breathability
- traps odor
- builds up over time

It's masking, not cleaning.

---

## The better option

White distilled vinegar.

The basic kind.

---

## Why it works

Vinegar:

- breaks down residue
- removes odors
- softens naturally

Clothes feel cleaner.

Lighter.

Less processed.

---

## How I use it

Replace softener with vinegar.

Pour into the softener compartment.

If you're me, add an extra rinse.

No real reason. Just feels right.

---

## What to buy

White Distilled Vinegar  
Glass Utility Bottle (optional)

Clean isn't a scent.
